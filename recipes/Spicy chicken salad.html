Spicy Chicken Salad

Description

A spicy chicken salad, made with a complex dressing including chili oil and sesame.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp sesame Paste
  • 2 tbsp chili oil
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 2 tsp sugar
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 1 tbsp sesame seeds
  • 2 spring onions, chopped
  • Cooked chicken
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 300g of cabbage, finely chopped

Steps

  1. Mix all the dressing ingredients well.
  2. Combine the chicken, carrot, cabbage and spring onion, cover in the dressing, mix well and serve.
