Jerk Chicken marinade

Ingredients

Description

A simple marinade variation for jerk chicken. Hot, deeply spiced and perfect for the barbecue.

Ingredients

  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 3 spring onions
  • 2 Scotch bonnet chillies
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 tbsp five-spice
  • 1 tbsp all-spice berries, coarsely ground
  • 1 tbsp ground black pepper
  • 1 tsp dried thyme
  • 1 tsp ground nutmeg
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 120 ml of soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 2 chickens, quartered

Steps

  1. Add all the dry ingredients too a food processor and blend. Slowly add soy sauce and vegetable oil while blending. THen pour marinade on and leave overnight.
  2. grill hte chicken over a medium flame, turning often for around 35-40 mins. Then serve
