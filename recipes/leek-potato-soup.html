Leek and Potato soup

Description

A simple, winter warmer soup. Cheap, easy, healthy and can be made with locally grown ingredients. This version is low-fat and dairy free, made with the use of Cannellini beans. Dijon mustard, lemon juice, white wine vinegar lend it a lively sharpness. Best eaten with crusty bread.

Ingredients

  • 3 Tbsp of olive oil
  • 3 large leeks
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 1 tbsp of white wine vinegar
  • 1 litre of stock (chicken or vegetable)
  • 3 large potatoes, around 500g
  • 350g of Cannellini beans
  • 1/2 tsp of Dijon mustard

Steps

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large pot and sweat the seasoned leeks for 6 to 8 mins until soft.
  2. Add the garlic and cook for 2 mins, then add the white wine vinegar and cooks for 30 mins. Then add the stock, beans, chopped potatoes and cook for 30 mins.
  3. Let cool slightly, then blend and add a tbsp of olive oil, mustard and blend.
